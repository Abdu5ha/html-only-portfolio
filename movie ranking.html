My top 3 movies of all time

  1. Money Heast

    • Original Run:2017–2021
    • Genre:Crime, Thriller, Drama
    • Language:Spanish (Dubbed versions available in multiple languages)

    Plot Overview

    "Money Heist" (originally La Casa de Papel) follows a group of skilled criminals who, under the guidance of a mastermind known as The Professor, attempt the biggest heists in history. first at the Royal Mint of Spain and later at the Bank of Spain. Their goal? To print billions of euros while staying inside long enough to outsmart the police.
    The story is full of strategic mind games, emotional twists, betrayals and intense action making it one of the most gripping crime series ever.

  2. Game of Thrones

    • Original Run:2011–2019
    • Genre:Fantasy, Drama, Action
    • LanguageEnglish

    Plot Overview

    Based on George R.R. Martin's book series A Song of Ice and Fire, Game of Thrones (GoT) is set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos. The series follows multiple noble families fighting for the Iron Throne while an ancient supernatural threat, the White Walkers, slowly emerges in the North.
    The show is known for its political intrigue, shocking deaths, intense battles, and unforgettable characters.

  3. prison break

    • Original Run:2005–2009 (Seasons 1–4), 2017 (Season 5)
    • Genre:Action, Thriller, Crime, Drama
    • Language :English

    Plot Overview

    Prison Break follows Michael Scofield, a genius structural engineer, who deliberately gets himself imprisoned to break his wrongfully accused brother, Lincoln Burrows, out of Fox River State Penitentiary.
    With an elaborate escape plan tattooed on his body, Michael must navigate ruthless inmates, corrupt prison officials, and the mysterious "Company" —a secret organization behind Lincoln’s framing.
    Each season introduces new twists, betrayals, and high-stakesaction as Michael and his allies fight for their freedom.
